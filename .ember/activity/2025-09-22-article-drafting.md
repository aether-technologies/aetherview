## Topic 3: Biometric Authentication in the Metaverse - Perplexity.ai Research

Completed initial research using Perplexity.ai for Topic 3. Key findings include:

- **Fundamental Concept**: Biometric authentication in the metaverse uses unique biological traits (facial recognition, fingerprints, eye tracking) to verify identities in virtual environments.

- **Privacy Considerations**: Biometric data in the metaverse is highly sensitive and immutable, raising concerns about data breaches and unauthorized surveillance. Transparency about collection, consent, and secure storage are essential.

- **Immersion Benefits**: Biometric data enables more personalized and realistic experiences through dynamic adaptation based on user emotions and behaviors.

- **Security Framework**: Combining biometrics with blockchain offers enhanced security through decentralized identity management and encryption.

- **Challenges**: Reliability issues (false positives/negatives), hardware dependencies, and ethical concerns around consent and surveillance remain significant hurdles.

Sources included Cointelegraph, Business at OECD, HKT Enterprise, and others discussing the balance between security, privacy, and immersive experiences.
